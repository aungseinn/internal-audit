Why Clear Delegation Matters

In a fast-paced retail environment, a clear delegation structure within the Audit Department is crucial. It ensures efficiency, accountability, and allows the team to effectively identify and manage risks, from head-office strategy down to operational processes.

Corporate Governance Focus

Audit Manager Reporting Line

The Audit Manager is strategically responsible for reporting the final audit findings and risk assessments directly to the MD (Audit Committee) for strategic oversight and governance compliance.

Delegation Flow (Internal Department)

Authority flows from the strategic oversight level down through the Audit Department for tactical execution, ensuring clear delegation at every stage.

Key Retail Considerations: Audit Area Delegation

To cover all company risks, the audit program includes these key functional areas, each with specific delegation rules.

Inventory

Stock count plans are assigned by the Senior Audit and must be approved by the Audit Manager.

Cash Management

Authority to perform cash counts is delegated to Junior/Senior Audits, with random checks conducted by the Audit Manager.

Compliance

Policy and regulatory adherence checks are executed by Junior Audit under the review of Senior Audit.

Supplier Management

Vendor contract and payment term review scope is determined by the Senior Audit, reported to the Manager.

Sales & Returns

Sales revenue cut-off and return process integrity testing is executed by Junior Audit for Senior Audit review.

Payable

Invoice verification and three-way matching review is done by Senior Audit, with Manager spot checks.

Receivable

Aged debtors analysis is performed by Senior Audit for Manager reporting on major risks.

Fixed Asset

Physical existence verification is delegated to Junior Audit, with reconciliation by Senior Audit.

HR & Admin (Incl. Payroll)

Sensitive payroll and key HR compliance verification is executed by Senior Audit and reviewed/approved by the Audit Manager.

Expense

Petty cash and general expense verification are handled by Junior Audit. Major expense voucher checks are executed by Senior Audit for Manager review.

Audit Follow-up

Responsibility for validating corrective actions is delegated to the Senior Audit, with overall monitoring by the Audit Manager.
